# D442 – Basic Nursing Skills: Complete Course Overview

---

## COURSE DESCRIPTION

Basic Nursing Skills introduces foundational principles of the nursing process and the clinical judgment model, health assessment techniques, and communication skills for diverse adult patients across the lifespan. Skills focus on vital signs, medication administration, infection control, nutrition, elimination, mobility, oxygenation, and skin integrity. **Students must be successful on lab assessments to progress to Adult Health 1 clinical.**

**Co-requisites:** D443 – Health Assessment; D444 – Adult Health I
**Prerequisite:** All prelicensure nursing curriculum courses from previous terms.

## COURSE ASSESSMENTS (What You Must Pass)

These are the **required assessments** to demonstrate competency for this course:

1. **Basic Nursing Skills Pre-Lab HESI Patient Review** (must score 80%+, due **two weeks before** first lab day)
2. **Basic Nursing Skills Pre-Lab Shadow Health Digital Clinical Experience (DCE)** (must score 80%+, due two weeks before first lab day)
3. **Basic Nursing Skills On Ground Lab** (Performance Assessment – must pass to progress to D444 Adult Health I Clinical Intensive)
